GNITS (G. Narayanamma Institute of Technology and Sciences) - Complete College Information

=== ADMISSIONS ===
UG Admissions: Candidates must qualify TG-EAPCET examination. Eligibility: 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry.
PG Admissions: Based on GATE examination. If GATE candidates unavailable, TS-PGECET merit considered.

=== FEE STRUCTURE ===
UG Fees: Category-A and Category-B (JEE): Rs.1,62,000 per annum + JNTUH fees
UG Fees NRI: USD 5,000 + JNTUH fees per annum
PG Fees: Rs.1,12,000 for both Category-A and Category-B

=== PROGRAMS OFFERED ===
B.Tech Programs: CSE, IT, ECE, EEE, Data Science, AI & ML
M.Tech Programs: Available in various specializations
